Shot Change Detection
Functionalities
Detects abrupt and gradual scene transitions in videos and divides them into structured segments with timestamps. Generates scene-based metadata that can be used for indexing, navigation, and automated video processing.
Applications
  • Automatic video editing in media production where long videos are split into meaningful scenes for faster editing.

  • Content summarization systems that generate highlights from sports, movies, or lectures.

  • Broadcast monitoring systems that automatically detect advertisement boundaries in TV streams.

Face Detection
Functionalities
Identifies and locates human faces frame by frame while assigning confidence scores for accuracy. Tracks faces across the video timeline and generates timestamp-based face appearance data.
Applications
  • Attendance and identity verification systems in classrooms, offices, and events.

  • Surveillance systems that monitor human presence and trigger alerts for unknown individuals.

  • Media analytics platforms that measure screen time and presence of people in interviews or films.

Object Detection
Functionalities
Detects and classifies multiple objects within video frames using trained models. Tracks object movement across timestamps and outputs label-based analytical data.
Applications
  • Retail and warehouse monitoring systems that track products and packaged goods in storage areas.

  • Smart traffic systems that detect vehicles and pedestrians for congestion analysis

  • Content moderation platforms that identify sensitive or restricted objects in uploaded videos.

Logo Detection
Functionalities
Recognizes predefined brand logos within video frames and determines their presence duration. Generates timestamp-based logo frequency and exposure analytics for reporting purposes.
Applications
  • Brand monitoring systems that track logo appearances in advertisements and social media videos.

  • Sponsorship analytics platforms that measure brand visibility during sports broadcasts.

  • Intellectual property protection systems that detect unauthorized brand usage online.
